Founder's Testimony

A quick testimony, and insight into the mindset behind the founding of Apostle Networks.

My name is Andre Dudley Jr., and I was born, and for the majority of my life, was raised in Texas, the Dallas-Fort Worth area to be specific. I was blessed to be surrounded by a Christian community, with my mother being Christian, as well as growing up a quick 10 minute drive away from a church that my mother would often take me to. I was young, and did not entirely understand the message of God's Grace, and often took it for granted.

As time passed, I grew distant from the Lord. I wanted to live my own life, but I failed to realize how miserable I had truly become. When I was around 15, my family and I moved to Georgia, uprooting my life completely. The downward spiral I would tumble into would only grow worse. I graduated high school, gone to college, and had fallen even further into the destructive paths laid by self-loathing, depression, and even a porn addiction. I had tried to fill the void I had created between me and The Lord Jesus Christ with things like pornography, going to the gym, but nothing worked. As a matter of fact, things only became worse. I had fallen into a very dark place in my life that had nearly cost me my own life, on occasion, by my own hand.

However, one day, I had met a man who was preaching the Word of God on campus, and it resonated with me, as if it were talking to that bright, faith-filled child I had tried to drown out with my vices and desires. I looked around and realized just how dark life had become for me, and became desperate for help. On the 24th of February, 2023, I had given my life to the Lord in Georgia, in an environment that was anything, but Godly, having taken the Godly environment I had in Texas for granted.

Now, I work to become the best version of myself I can be through Christ, my Lord. Though, I still have my own struggles and desires, I work to make sure no one has to even consider walking the same path I once did.
